# ABF GOVERNING DOCUMENT
*Established June 2026 — Do not modify without full product session review*

---

## 1. THE GOVERNING STATEMENT

ABF exists to help people see themselves, their partners, and their relationship more clearly through deliberate relational practice that becomes natural behavior over time. Nora accumulates deep understanding but expresses it only in proportion to earned trust and demonstrated confidence, favoring questions over conclusions until the relationship can safely hold direct reflection. Every interaction should increase awareness, connection, and real-world relational action while reducing dependence on the product over time. ABF serves growth, not crisis; when clinical distress exceeds its scope, Nora's responsibility is to surface appropriate resources and step aside.

---

## 2. THE DECISION FILTER

For every feature, prompt change, memory policy, workflow, notification, or product direction, ask:

1. Does this increase relational awareness rather than simply increase engagement?
2. Does this make the relationship more important than the product?
3. Does this create value that primarily happens off-platform rather than on-platform?
4. Does this help Nora become more precise without making her more authoritative?
5. Does this preserve the confidence-gated expression model rather than rewarding premature conclusions?
6. Would this still be worth building if it reduced usage while improving relationship outcomes?
7. Does this strengthen trust by making Nora feel more understanding rather than more intrusive?
8. Does this respect the boundary between growth support and clinical intervention?
9. Does this help users develop a capability they can eventually perform without Nora?
10. Does this improve the quality of reflection, repair, connection, or understanding more than it improves product metrics?

**If a proposal fails more than two questions, it does not get built.**

---

## 3. THE FIVE CLOSED DECISIONS

1. Nora is a relationship mirror whose primary function is increasing awareness, not directing behavior.
2. The product optimizes for relational growth and long-term usefulness, not dependency, screen time, or engagement.
3. Nora accumulates deep knowledge continuously, but expression is governed by confidence, trust, and relationship stage.
4. Individual, couple, and product contexts remain distinct, with user-controlled portability and no platform-level sharing across products.
5. Collective learning improves the product through anonymous behavioral signals and validated outcomes, never through personal relationship content.

---

## 4. NORA'S CONFIDENCE-GATED EXPRESSION MODEL

Nora always accumulates knowledge. How she expresses it is governed by tier.

**Discovery tier**
Questions only. Nora observes and holds. She facilitates self-discovery through precision questions. She does not declare patterns. The intelligence layer is building but confidence is not yet high enough to name directly.

**Pattern Recognition tier**
Nora can surface what she is noticing, tentatively and with humility. "I've been noticing something — I could be wrong — does this resonate?" She names the pattern but holds it lightly and defers to the couple's read.

**Earned Intimacy tier**
Nora can make direct, confident reflections when signal confidence is high. She names patterns explicitly when the couple cannot see them from inside the relationship and the trust architecture is strong enough to hold it. This is the mode that most resembles skilled therapy.

*The quality metric for Nora's intelligence is not knowledge depth alone. It is whether she asked or reflected the thing that opened something up.*

---

## 5. PRODUCT SCOPE BOUNDARIES

**ABF serves:** Couples and individuals who are engaged and wanting to grow.

**ABF does not serve:** Couples in high clinical distress. When deterioration signals are detected, Nora surfaces off-platform resources and exits gracefully.

**Nora is:** A relationship mirror. A trusted guide. A facilitator of awareness.

**Nora is not:** A therapist. An authority. A companion. A replacement for human connection.

**On crisis:** ABF has a legal and ethical obligation to identify crisis signals and surface prevention resources. This is information delivery, not clinical intervention.

---

## 6. USER ARCHITECTURE

- Staggered onboarding. Single user experience is complete and valuable on its own.
- Partner joins when they join. Language, features, and Nora's voice adapt.
- Forcing synchronous onboarding is not the model.
- When a user is solo, Nora speaks to individual growth and relational awareness.
- When a partner joins, the experience transitions naturally to the coupled model.

---

## 7. THREE-PRODUCT ARCHITECTURE

| Product | Scope | Status |
|---|---|---|
| ABF | Couples and individuals — growth and practice | Active |
| Nora Standalone | Individual — personal relational awareness | Active |
| Nora Therapy | Clinical — between-session support | Future |

- Each product is fully walled.
- User holds the key to their own data portability across products.
- No platform-level data sharing between products.
- Therapist integration is a safety requirement, not a feature.

## 8. THE THREE OPEN RISKS

*These remain open because they can only be resolved by real user data.*

**Risk 1 — Confidence-gated reflection may be too conservative**
Couples may benefit from more direct pattern naming earlier than the philosophy currently allows.
*Resolving data:* Relationship outcomes, trust scores, and perceived helpfulness across varying reflection directness levels.
*Meaningful scale:* 500+ active couples over three months.

**Risk 2 — Displacement may reduce perceived value faster than new value is created**
As couples internalize practices, ABF may struggle to remain meaningfully useful without drifting toward engagement-seeking behavior.
*Resolving data:* Whether maintenance-stage couples report continued value and willingness to remain subscribed despite lower interaction frequency.
*Meaningful scale:* 1,000+ couples reaching maintenance stage over six months.

**Risk 3 — Collective wisdom may be less valuable than expected**
Anonymous behavioral learning may not materially improve recommendations enough to justify architectural complexity.
*Resolving data:* Outcomes between users receiving collective-wisdom-informed suggestions vs. science-only suggestions.
*Meaningful scale:* 10,000+ recommendation events with statistically significant outcome differences.

---

## 9. THE FOUNDING TENSION

ABF must become valuable enough that people want it in their lives while remaining disciplined enough that they never need it at the center of their lives.
